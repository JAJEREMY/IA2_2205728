Academic Reflection

a. Showcase how your strongest trait and emotion, hobby, etc. impact your school/academic life.

My strongest trait is perseverance, which has greatly impacted my academic journey. I consistently push through challenges, such as difficult assignments and programming tasks, by seeking additional resources or consulting with professors and peers. This trait allows me to excel even in subjects where I initially struggle, making sure I finish strong.

Application to My Life:

I will continue to utilize my perseverance in my academic life by pushing through any challenges in my courses, especially in programming, where I tend to encounter difficult tasks. By remaining determined, I will ensure my success in this field.

I will keep this quote in mind as I navigate through my academic journey. Even if faced with challenges, especially in difficult subjects like mathematics or coding, I will remind myself that persistence is key to achieving my goals.
